\subsection*{Problem 23}
\begin{itemize}
    \item[(a)] I'll leave the drawing to you but here are  the key equations:

          Inverse demand: $P = 11 - 2Q$, intercept $11$. \\
          Inverse supply: $P = Q + 2$, intercept $2$.
    \item[(b)] From $5.5 - 0.5P = P - 2$: \quad $\boxed{P^* = 5}, \; Q^* = 3$.
    \item[(c)] At $P = 9$: $Q_d=1$, $Q_s=7$ \quad $\Rightarrow \quad \boxed{Q=1}$.
    \item[(d)] With tax: $P = Q + 5$, solve $11 - 2Q = Q + 5$: \quad $\boxed{Q=2}, \; P_c=7, \; P_s=4$.
\end{itemize}
